Culture

Discover the rich cultural heritage of Morocco. Each part of the culture is a reflection of Morocco's vibrant history.

  • Music: Traditional Moroccan music with influences from Arabic, Berber, and Sub-Saharan African traditions.
  • Food: Moroccan cuisine, including tagine, couscous, and traditional sweets like baklava.
  • Art: Unique Moroccan arts, including mosaics, pottery, and calligraphy.
  • Architecture: Morocco's famous architecture, like the Hassan II Mosque, kasbahs, and riads.
  • Clothes: Traditional Moroccan attire, such as the djellaba and caftan.
